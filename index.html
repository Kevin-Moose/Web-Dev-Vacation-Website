Come and discover the hidden Mediterranean paradise of Albania. A small overlooked country sandwiched between Italy and Greece, Albania has many beautiful areas worth visiting. From the breathtaking mountains in the North, to the quiet and peaceful beaches in the South you are guaranteed to be impressed and left shocked as to why you never thought to visit here before.

With culture similar to that of the neighboring countries it provides many things that most people already love about the Mediterranean. Fresh delicious food, historical monuments, rich history, and landscapes only thought of in dreams. Combined with the hospitality that Albania is renowned for you will feel like an honored guest wherever you go.

There are many beautiful beaches along Albania's 224 mile (362km) coastline. All the way from Durres in the North to Saranda in the South. Best of all these beaches are never crowded, and many are just waiting to just be explored.

And if you prefer mountains instead of beaches than Albania has got you covered. Explore many vibrant and jaw dropping mountains with peaks that pierce the sky open. The village of Theth has recently become a hotspot for hikers and travelers in recent years for its isolated location high up in the Northern mountains.
