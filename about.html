Hello everybody! My name is Kevin M. I was born in Athens, Greece to ethnic Albanian parents from the Southeastern city of Korca, Albania. I lived most of my childhood in Athens before I migrated to the US in 2001. Although I moved miles and miles away from the region I called home I never forgot my roots, and culture. I love my home country of Albania, and the history that it has gone through. For many milleniums they have survived countless empires, assimilations, and invasions, and through it all it still remained. This is why Albanians tend to be very patriotic and expressive over the love of their country and this is why I decided to make a website about this beautiful hidden gem in the Mediterranean. I hope you consider it on your bucket list in the future. Thank you!
